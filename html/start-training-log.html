Athletes, Start Tracking Your Training!

Get started with your own training log in just a few minutes
Use AthleticAPP to track your workouts!

AthleticAPP is the heart of your Athletic experience! Use it to communicate with your coaches and teammates, follow your favorite athletes, log workouts of any kind, and receive and complete coach-assigned workouts.


1.  Download AthleticAPP

AthleticAPP is available for both iOS and Android devices and is a free download! Not only will it allow you to receive assigned workouts from your coaches and log your own workouts, but it allows you to compete in virtual meets and communicate with your team. Download it using the links above.

2.  Create an Account

Signing up for a free account only takes a few minutes and can be done right from within the app. Make sure you select your matching name from similar names to associate your account with your performance history.

3.  Log a Workout

Record your first workout by hitting the Add Post icon () from the bottom of the screen, then choose Record Workout. Choose an activity, and get out there! Your time and distance will automatically be tracked and saved by AthleticAPP and added to your log!
