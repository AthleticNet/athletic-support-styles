Track Athlete Progress
Athlete Profiles and results for each season

Curious what someone's best 200M time was last season? Go to their athlete bio and look at their records for each event sorted by season. Scroll down to see every single recorded result for their entire running career. Every Season Record and Personal Record is marked so you can quickly see their improvement. You can find an athlete's profile page by clicking his name in any results or rankings page or by using the search tool at the top of every page.

Coaches can assign workouts to athletes, for which they can fill in the details as they complete the workout. This is a great way to keep the entire team up to date on everyone's training progress! Talk to your coach about this feature if he's not yet using it to assign your workouts.
